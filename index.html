Active HOME funded properties in Ohio.

Generally, landlords operating HOME-funded properties may only evict a tenant for good cause and after a thirty-day notice.
Tenants and legal services attorneys often have difficulty identifying which properties are subject to the HOME restrictions. The below map and database search helps advocates quickly determine if a rental unit is subject to the HOME restrictions.

All addresses were collected from the National Housing Preservation Database (NHPD). The addresses mapped below were accurate as of July, 2014.

Instructions:

  • Click on any point to see more information including the name and address of the project.
  • Or, enter the name of the project in the query below the map to isolate a single point.
  • NOTE: Some points represent a "scattered site" project, meaning that the point represents multiple units at different addresses throughout the surrounding area.

Disclaimer: Tenants and advocates should be sure to obtain the restrictive covenant from the county recorder to confirm the existence and status of the restriction. As with any data collected from the NHPD, the information is not gauranteed to be 100% accurate.
